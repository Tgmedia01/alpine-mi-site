(01) — The brief

The second studio — brick, warm steel, programme.

Twelve weeks after handing over Cornhill, Solo Sixty asked us to build their second site on Hanbury Street in Spitalfields. The shell was a former workshop — exposed brick walls, steel I-beams, and an awkward column grid we left in place.

The brief was to make the column grid part of the room, not fight it. We picked up the visual rhythm with a black steel railing system and a feature lighting plot tracked to the columns.

(02) — FabricBrick + steel

Keep the workshop

We took the building back to its skin: every brick wall left exposed, lime-pointed where loose, and clear-sealed. The steel beams above were shot-blasted on site and left raw. Nothing was painted that did not need to be.

(03) — Repeat2nd site · 12 weeks on

Same team, second site

The Cornhill team moved straight onto Hanbury — same site manager, same joiners, same M&E lead. Programme compression of 20% versus Cornhill on a like-for-like scope, with shared kit and pre-fabbed components from our workshop.
