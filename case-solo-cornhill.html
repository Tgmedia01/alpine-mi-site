(01) — The brief

A boutique fitness studio inside a trading City building.

Solo Sixty approached us with a tight brief: a full studio fit-out on the lower ground floor of a Cornhill office building, with offices and a café trading live on the floors above and beside.

Acoustic isolation, weekend delivery windows and a fixed launch date drove the programme. The studio opened on plan with zero noise complaints in the first 30 days of trade.

(02) — AcousticFloat + isolators

A floating floor

A 75mm sprung floor on neoprene isolators decoupled the studio from the structural slab. Wall and ceiling linings were detailed with resilient bars and double plasterboard. Impact noise through the floor was reduced by 24 dB measured.

(03) — Out-of-hoursWknd + nights

Built around the trading day

The base building traded Monday to Friday. We worked Saturdays, Sundays and weeknights from 19:00, with a dedicated goods lift slot and a daily clean-down to leave the route clear for tenants by 06:00. Zero late starts on the trading floor above.
