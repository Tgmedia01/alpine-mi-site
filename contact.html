(07) — Frequently asked

Before you write

How fast will you reply?

Most enquiries get a phone reply within one working day. If the brief is in detail, a director will follow up with a written response inside 48 hours and propose a site visit.

What size projects do you take on?

Commercial fit-outs typically range from £25k–£2m.;Residential and design & build projects range from £2k–£2m. Smaller specialist works, such as joinery or period refurbishments, are considered on a case-by-case basis.

Do I need drawings to enquire?

No. Sketches, briefs, mood-boards or a phone call are enough. If you already have an architect, we'll work alongside them from Stage 2 onwards.

Do you work outside London?

Routinely across the South East. Further afield is considered for repeat clients and multi-site programmes.

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes — happily, for sensitive briefs. Send us yours or ask for ours.
