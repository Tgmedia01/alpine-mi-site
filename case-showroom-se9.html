(01) — The brief

A trade showroom for a returning fit-out client.

A trade client we had worked with on hospitality projects asked us to fit out their new customer-facing showroom in Eltham — a clean, lit room to receive contractors and specifiers, with a back-of-house workshop and material library.

We delivered the shell-and-core conversion and the full interior in one programme, with a display system our workshop built to swap out as the product range changes.

(02) — DisplaySwap system

A display rig that moves with the range

The display walls are a steel rail-and-bracket system on a 200mm module — our workshop built and powder-coated all the components. Product can be re-merchandised by the client team in under a day; no contractor call-out needed.

(03) — WorkshopIntegrated BoH

A showroom and a workshop, one programme

Behind the showroom is a 60m² workshop where the client preps and finishes their own product. We coordinated dust extraction, three-phase power and a goods door into the existing yard — built alongside the customer-facing rooms in a single 14-week programme.
